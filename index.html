Why CFA?

Every shape tweak means waiting on CFD — hours to days of supercomputer time and a specialized engineering team. CFA screens designs in seconds, so you can iterate freely and save full CFD runs for your shortlisted candidates.

Parameter-based baseline

26 design parameters in, LightGBM/XGBoost out. Fast, tabular, no 3D file required.

Shape-aware prediction

Upload an STL/OBJ and a point-cloud deep model (PointNet) reads the actual geometry.

Confidence you can trust

Every prediction ships with an expected error band (MAE / R²) from the official DrivAerNet++ test split.

Actionable suggestions

When Cd runs high, CFA points to the specific geometry — bumper, ramp angle, roofline — driving it up.
